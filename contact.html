Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How long does shipping take?

We process orders within 24 hours. Shipping times vary based on location: Metro cities typically receive orders in 2-3 days, while other locations take 4-7 business days. You'll receive a tracking number once your order ships.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely happy with your purchase, contact us within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. Products must be unopened and in original packaging for returns.

Do you offer wholesale pricing?

Yes! We offer special pricing for retailers, spas, and wellness centers. Please email wholesale@goreze.in with your business details and expected order volume for our wholesale catalog and pricing information.

How should I store Goreze?

Store unopened bottles in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Once opened, refrigerate and consume within 24 hours for maximum freshness and potency. The natural ingredients are best enjoyed fresh!
